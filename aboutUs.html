About Us

Welcome to Camerado RV & Boat Storage, your premier storage solution for recreational vehicles and boats in the heart of El Dorado Hills. We offer secure, spacious, and easily accessible storage options designed to protect your investment year-round. With state-of-the-art security, wide drive aisles, and both covered and uncovered storage options, we make storing your RV or boat convenient and worry-free. Our locally owned and operated facility is committed to providing top-tier customer service, ensuring that your adventures start and end with ease. Whether you're heading to the lake, the mountains, or the open road, trust us to keep your RV or boat safe until your next great journey.
